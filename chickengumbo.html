Chicken Gumbo

Ingredients

  • 3 T. butter (or olive oil)
  • 1/4 C. chopped green bell pepper
  • 1-1/2 C. chopped okra
  • 1/4 C chopped onion
  • 1 Qt good chicken stock
  • 2-1/2 C. stewed tomatoes
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • parsley
  • 1 pound boneless skinless chicken breast diced or shredded
  • 1/2 pound small shrimp peeled deveined and cooked (optional(

Cooking Instructions

  1. in a soup pot, heat butter
  2. add chicken, cook until it starts to brown
  3. add green pepper, onion and okra
  4. simmer about 10 minutes or until lightly browned
  5. add chicken stock and tomatoes
  6. boil gently until vegetables are tender
  7. season to taste
  8. add shrimp and parsley
  9. note: I add cooked rice
  10. I don't know why this recipe doesn't start with a roux ...it's a little lighter than traditional gumbo without it but if you want it a little richer - before you add the veggies, remove meat and make the roux (until it's nice and brown) then add the meat back to the roux and continue with the recipe
  11. you could also add sausage (such as andouille) to do this add sliced sausage to the pot with the chicken to brown
