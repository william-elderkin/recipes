Chicken Parm Meatballs

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs ground chicken
  • 3/4 c. panko
  • 1/4 c. minced or dried onion
  • 2 T. parsley
  • 2 T basil
  • 3-4 cloves garlic
  • 3/4 c. shredded pecorino romano or pamesan
  • 1/2 tsp. freshly ground pepper
  • 1/4 c. plus 2 c. marinara sauce (divided)
  • 4-6 oz. mozzarella or provolone - sliced or shredded

Cooking Instructions

  1. pre-heat oven to 350ยบ and line a baking sheet with parchment
  2. set aside the mozzarella/provolone and 2 c. marinara
  3. in a large bowl, combine all remaining ingredients
  4. mix well and roll into small (cocktail size) balls
  5. bake for about 20 minutes..until lightly browned
  6. turn oven to broil
  7. drizzle marinara over each meatball and top with cheese
  8. broil for 2-3 minutes...until cheese is golden and bubbly yum!
