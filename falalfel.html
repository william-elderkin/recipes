Falalfel Patties

Ingredients

  • 2 T olive oil
  • 1-1/2 C minced onion
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 C grated carrot
  • 1-3/4 C (15oz can) cooked chickpeas (drained)
  • 1-1/2 T tahini
  • 1/4 C minced fresh parsley
  • 1/3 C chickpea flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Cooking Instructions

  1. In a large skillet over medium heat, sautee onions in 1 T olive oil until soft
  2. Add garlic, cumin and carrot then sautee 2 minutes.
  3. Transfer to a large bowl.
  4. Add drained chickpeas, mash by hand or food processor, add tahini and parsley.
  5. In a smaller bowl combine chickpea flour, baking soda and.
  6. Add to veggie mixture and mix well.
  7. With floured hands form 4 patties and lightly dust with remaining flour.
  8. Heat 1 T oil in large skillet over medium heat(or on a griddle).
  9. Add patties to skillet.
  10. Cook for 1 minute or when browning starts, then flip.
  11. Cook for about 2 minutes before flipping again.
  12. Keep cooking and flipping every minute or 2 until they are a deep golden brown on both sides.
  13. Squeeze fresh lemon juice on top. (I never remember to do this)
