twice baked sweet potato casserole

Ingredients

  • 4 good size sweet potatoes
  • 1/2 c. light brown sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp. kosher salt
  • 1/2 stick butter - separated
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts..such as pecans or walnuts

Cooking Instructions

  1. pre-heat oven to 375ยบ
  2. bake the sweet potatoes until tender..about an hour...maybe a little longer* remove from oven and let them cool a bit before peeling mash peeled potatoes with the cinnamon, salt, a couple tablespoons of the brown sugar and 1/4 stick of butter...scoop into a greased baking dish mix together the remaining brown sugar, butter and chopped nuts and sprinkle over sweet potato mixture
  3. bake for about 20-25 minutes
  4. * you could also roast the sweet potatoes...this would add a bit of caramelized flavor to the finished product (just peel, cube, drizzle with a bit of oil, sprinkle with salt and roast until caramelized and tender) ...if you do this just skip the salt when you do the mashing..I would also probably skip adding the butter to the mash as well since the potatoes already got a shot of oil but beware...sweet potatoes roasted like this are highly addictive!! ...you could easily eat them all before ya get a chance to make your yummy casserole hahaha
